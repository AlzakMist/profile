SmartVest

Capstone · Published, IJRSI 2026

An IoT-based smart vest built to monitor the real-time health and environmental safety of traffic enforcers. Sensor data streams from an ESP32 to a Flutter app over Firebase, with automatic SOS alerts and GPS coordinates pushed to registered guardians and emergency responders the moment a reading crosses a safe threshold.

  • Heart rate, body temperature, fall detection, air quality, humidity, and GPS fused into one live dashboard with alert history
  • Guardian contact management with automatic SOS alerts, successfully demonstrated for real-time emergency detection
  • Capstone research project at Jesus Reigns Christian College, published in the International Journal of Research and Scientific Innovation
